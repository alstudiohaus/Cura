How it works

Four steps. One prompt. Done.

01
🔗

Connect Drive

Link your Google Drive. Cura indexes your entire photo library — faces, scenes, moods, objects — without moving a single file.

02
✍️

Describe your reel

Type naturally. "5 clips of food and travel from Bali, warm tones" or just "friends and laughter." The AI understands intent, not just keywords.

03
🧠

AI curates & sequences

Claude vision scores every photo against your brief. It picks the strongest 10–30, arranges them for narrative flow, and applies transitions.

04
📱

Export & post

Download a 9:16 MP4 ready for Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube Shorts. Or push directly to your content queue.

Capabilities

Built for creators who think in stories, not files

👁️

Scene-aware vision

Every photo is understood: location, mood, objects, faces, lighting quality, and aesthetic score. Not just tags — contextual meaning.

✦

Intent-based selection

Describe what you want in plain language. The AI reasons about which photos serve that story — the way a human creative director would.

📐

Narrative sequencing

Photos are arranged for flow — visual contrast, pacing, and emotional arc — not just chronologically or randomly.

🎵

Mood-matched music

Royalty-free tracks suggested based on your brief and pacing. Beat-sync transitions cut automatically to the drop.

🔗

Google Drive native

Photos stay in your Drive. Cura reads and indexes without copying files — your storage, your control.

♾️

Permutation engine

Generate 5 different reel variations from the same prompt — different selections, orderings, and vibes — then pick your favourite.
